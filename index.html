Our story

Fine jewelry since 1984

Gamma Creations combines traditional craftsmanship, a respect for time-honoured techniques, with cutting edge technology to create pieces where care and devotion are reflected in every facet. Each creation is produced entirely in-house, never leaving our workshop studio until it is sent to the customer.

By totally controlling the production process at every step we ensure the very highest standards of quality and service.
